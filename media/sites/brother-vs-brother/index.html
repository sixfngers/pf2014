THE BROTHER VS. BROTHER DIGITAL DISPLAY CAMPAIGN

Season 2 Called for Superheroes

It started as a competition as Season 1 pitted the brothers head-to-head – #TeamDrew vs. #TeamJonathan. This time, the focus was to position them as action heroes, a super team capable of tackling the most incredible renovations.

GREEN SCREEN SET THEM FREE

To bring the ad concept to life, we began by designing and producing a green screen shoot that captured the Scotts acting out a series of “superhero” moves.


bpg’s Chief Creative Officer Steph Sebbag directed the shoot, while our EVP Interactive, Andy Robbins, made sure the footage would seamlessly transition to our banners, offering an array of actions to work with various units.

REAL-LIFE ACTION TO DIGITAL MAGIC

From soaring across the air to grabbing a hammer, smashing through a wall or dropping through units to land effortlessly, bpg composited the Scotts’ action into a high impact campaign of synched roadblocks, billboards and takeovers.
